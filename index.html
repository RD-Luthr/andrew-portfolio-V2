About Me

Passion for Data Science and Business

Pursuing a B.S. in Data Science and AI at the University of Miami, I specialize in data analysis, programming, and web development. With minors in Entrepreneurship and Mathematics, I integrate technical expertise with business insights.

At PadStats, I designed AI-powered analytics tools and user interfaces for real estate professionals, enhancing decision-making processes. As a technical analyst at Bonsai Applied Computations Group, I develop UI/UX front-end solutions and algorithms across neuroscience, marketing, and finance, leveraging data-driven insights to drive innovation.

My technical expertise spans Python, R, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and SQL with certifications in Microsoft Azure AI and Bloomberg Market Concepts. Beyond work, I love to exercise, listen to podcasts, read non-fiction books, and continuously develop my skills while exploring business-driven tech innovations, intuitive UI design, and cutting-edge applications of machine learning and data visualization.
